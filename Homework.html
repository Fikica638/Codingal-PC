Welcome to Ljuben Lape School

About the School

Ljuben Lape School, located in the heart of Skopje, is a prestigious educational institution known for its long-standing commitment to academic excellence and community engagement.

With a heritage spanning over a century, this school has evolved significantly while preserving its core values. It serves a diverse student body and offers a wide range of programs designed to cater to the educational needs of its students.

  • Established: The school has a rich history, dating back to its founding in the early 20th century.
  • Size: The school covers a large campus with modern facilities and ample space for various activities.
  • Student Body: Over 1200 students are currently enrolled, representing a vibrant and dynamic learning community.
  • Facilities: The school features state-of-the-art classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, and recreational areas.

The school's mission is to foster a holistic development approach, ensuring that each student reaches their full potential both academically and personally.

Historical Significance

Founded in the early 20th century, Ljuben Lape has a legacy of nurturing future leaders and innovators. Throughout its history, the school has adapted to educational advancements while maintaining a deep-rooted connection to its traditions.

Its long history is a testament to its enduring commitment to providing quality education and contributing to the community.
